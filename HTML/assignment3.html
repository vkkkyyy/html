CITIES

Nairobi city

  • Capital city of Kenya
  • Became Kenya's capital in 1907, replacing Mombasa.
  • A key starting point for safaris, earning it the title "Safari Capital of the World

new york city

  1. The city is composed of five distinct districts: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, and Staten Island
  2. New York City served as the first capital of the United States from 1785 to 1790
  3. Its the most populous city in the United States and serves as a global hub for finance, culture, and media

Paris

  • known as the City of Light
  • Is the capital of France
  • It's a global center for art, fashion, and history.

London

  1. is the capital and largest city of both England and the United Kingdom
  2. It is also the city with the highest population in the UK. The population is just under 9 million.

Tokyo

  1. Tokyo began life as a village known as Edo.

  3. Tokyo has the worlds busiest metro system.
